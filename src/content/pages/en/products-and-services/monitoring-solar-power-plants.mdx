<p class="ico"><strong>Basic product for photovoltaic power station ownors and investor, who manage their power station by themselves.</strong>&nbsp;Through the Solarmon system, it is possible to monitor all the compononts of your power station and detect their current state. Solarmon also allows you to acquire summary of active and reactive power, so you can optimize your operation and thereby your financial profit.&nbsp;<img class="paragraph-ico" title="Monitoring Standard" src="\{#WEBROOT\}upload/images/ico_monitoring-blue.png" alt="obr.: Monitoring Standard" width="50" height="42" /></p>
<p class="ico">Product &ldquo;Monitoring&rdquo; doesn&rsquo;t involve output data analysis, which can be ordered independently through confirmed electronic order.</p>
<ul class="ico">
<li>Simple and well-arranged system</li>
<li>Localization of losses and efficiency drops</li>
<li>Definition of losses on individual parts of power station</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="offset-by-one five omega columns">
<ul>
<li>Meteorological measurement (light exposure sensor, wind speed, panel temperature)</li>
<li>Regulation of P and Q power</li>
<li>Monitoring of inverters (central, decentral)</li>
<li>Monitoring of strings, electrometers and main switchboard</li>
<li>Well-arranged bank reports</li>
<li>Link with distribution system</li>
<li>E-mail and sms reports</li>
<li>Monitoring system for multiple power stations</li>
<li>Automatic updates</li>
<li>Onlino access  to documentation</li>
</ul>
