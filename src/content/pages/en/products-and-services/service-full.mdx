<p class="ico" >Service FULL is <strong>complex service </strong>for <strong>securing operation </strong>and <strong>maintenance </strong>of photovoltaic power station. When purchased, power station monitoring, dispatch and service is fully transferred to Solarmonitoring Company, which fulfills them within strictly defined terms and conditions. Premium bundle of services is included within yearly fee and it is comprised of preventive spare parts reserve which minimizes servicing interval in case of malfunction, yearly <strong>thermo vision measurement </strong>and <strong>panel check</strong>, <strong>energy audit</strong>, <strong>green keeping </strong>and more. Key article of this bundle is <strong>guarantee of yearly power station production </strong>and <strong>insurance up to 800 000 EUR </strong>in case of low yearly production caused by technical difficulties. This insurance doesn&rsquo;t include low production caused by weather conditions. <img class="paragraph-ico" title="Monitoring Standard" src="/upload/images/ico_service-blue.png" alt="obr.: Monitoring Standard" width="51" height="42" /></p>
