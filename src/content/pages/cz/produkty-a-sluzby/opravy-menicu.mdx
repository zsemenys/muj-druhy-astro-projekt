<h2>Poz&aacute;ručn&iacute; opravy</h2>
<p>Prov&aacute;d&iacute;me poz&aacute;ručn&iacute; opravy po&scaron;kozen&yacute;ch, porouchan&yacute;ch zař&iacute;zen&iacute;.
